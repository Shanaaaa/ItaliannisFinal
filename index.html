About Us

Originally a concept started in 1991 by Carlson Restaurants, the parent company of TGI Friday’s. Carlson Restaurants grew this concept, opening multiple Italianni’s across the United States, as well as internationally. In 1996 the first Italianni’s opened in Mexico (San Ángel, México City) and also this year opened the first Italianni’s in Philippines, (Makati City). In 2002 Carlson sold Italianni’s Brand to Remigio S. de R.L, corporation that has been expanding the brand succesfully.
